ABOUT DEPARTMENT :

The department of chemistry was established in 1950. The department deals with curriculum in applied chemistry / material science common to all branches
in engineering.The department thus only deals with first year student is treated as minor one.Besides teaching activity the staff members of this department
are involved in teaching of some materials like alloy, cement, lime, alum, bleaching powder, water and aggregates. Also coordinate first year admission process
& other activities of the collage.In the last five years the faculty members of the department have contributed more than 30 research papers in internationa
journals of repute and international conference proceedings.

LABORATORIES :

The chemistry department at present bears no specialized laboratory. Only laboratory facilities required for practical sessions of first year degree & diploma students are available. The laboratory is spacious & is capable to accommodate degree & diploma batches simultaneously. At a single session about 70 students can perform practical. This laboratory is a general chemistry laboratory. However, the department has plans to expand the scope to the following :

  • ElectroChemistry, Nonconventional Energy and Corrosin Science Lab
  • Bio-Inorganic Chemistry Lab
  • Synthetic Organic Chemistry Lab
  • Molecular Recognition Lab
  • Natural Product Chemistry Lab
